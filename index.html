Regular Fit Jeans

These jeans are usually worn by guys who are not too big boned or thin.Regular fit jeans meaning having a mid-rise and has a large leg opening.

Skinny Jeans

These jeans snugly fit around the waist and are tight on the thighs and calves, tapering down to the ankle.

Relaxed Fit Jeans

These jeans are ideal for people who have thick thighs as it gives them a comfortable fit. It provides you with extra room around the waist.

Drop Crotch Jeans

These are slouchy, relaxed fit pants that are saggy at the hips, seat, and rear. This denim is perfect to carry a cool chic look!

Slim Fit Jeans

These are similar to skinny jeans that fit on the thighs till the calves and stretch till the ankles. This gives an ultimate chic fit.

Loose Fit

These jeans have a baggy fit which gives you breathing space. Loose jeans provide plenty of space around the thighs and butt so that it doesn't stick to your thighs too tight.
